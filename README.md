# 18mm Model Rocket Display Stand

![Alt text](https://cdn.hackclub.com/019f3050-fadb-7e66-90b6-e21c5d5dff9c/screenshot_2026-07-05_132516.png)

## Overview

My project is a display stand for an 18mm model rocket, keeping the rocket in a perfect condition while making it presentable. With this 3D print, the model rocket becomes a show piece along with a functional rocket!


## Features

- Quick to print and zero assembly required
- Angled Display style, keeping the rocket at an angle to simulate a launch position.
- 18mm Fit: Designed to hold standard 18mm model rocket motor mounts.
- Features a hexagonal base pattern to save 3D printing material and time.
- Designed with a wide, triangular base for stability.
- Minimalist Design with clean geometric lines suitable for a desk or shelf display.

## Parts

The Model Rocket Display Stand requires no assembly and is fully print in place. It is a one part peice.

## 3D Printing Settings

### Material :

PLA or PETG are best suited for this project, though most fillaments should work perfectly. No specific fillaments are required. Ensure fillament is completely dry before printing to ensure part sizing and fit, as bubbling may cause an uneven surface.

### Infill : 

15% infill recommended. Less infill should be okay, but nothing below 7% infill. Recommended infill type is Gyroid or Tri-Hexagon infill for higher strength.


### Outer Walls : 

3 outer walls recommended as the part is meant to be under constant use.

### Supports : 

No supports are required. Please print part flat on bed with the Hexagonal base side facing down.

## Assembly (Post processing)

### 1. 

Clean up the 3D printed parts (Remove stringing if visible) 

### 2.

The Model Rocket Display Stand is ready to use! Slide the bottom of your rocket onto the tube and place in desired location! 

NOTE: Depending on the height and top weight of your rocket, double sided tape or some other type of adhesive may be required to hold the stand down to stop it from falling over.
